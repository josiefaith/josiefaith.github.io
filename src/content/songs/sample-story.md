This is a sample story page — it only appears while running the site locally
(`draft: true` keeps it off the real site). It exists so you can see the shape
before writing a real one.

A good song story here might cover: where you were when you wrote it, the line
that came first, what it's actually about versus what people assume, a detail
from recording it. Anything you'd tell someone at an open mic if they asked
"what's that one about?"

To write a real one: copy `_template.md` in this folder, name it after the
song, and just talk.
